# 🍫 Chocolate Market Sales, Price-Pack & Promotion Analytics

## 📌 Overview

This end-to-end analytics project explores sales performance in the global chocolate market, focusing on brand performance, pricing strategy, product mix, promotion effectiveness, customer segmentation, and channel performance.

Using SQL and Power BI, the project transforms transactional sales data into actionable business insights that support revenue growth, profitability optimization, and strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

How can businesses optimize pricing, promotions, product mix, and channel strategies to drive profitable growth?

Key questions addressed:

- Which brands generate the highest revenue and profit?
- What product configurations deliver the strongest margins?
- How effective are promotions and discounts?
- Which customer segments contribute the most value?
- Which sales channels and markets offer the greatest growth opportunities?

## Promotion & Discount Analysis

<img src="images/promotion_discount_analysis.png" width="100%">

### Key Insights

- 74% of total units were sold under promotion.
- Promotional sales contributed 66% of total revenue.
- A 12% discount achieved the highest profit margin.
- Higher discounts increased purchase quantity but reduced profitability.

---

## Customer & Channel Analysis

<img src="images/customer_channel_analysis.png" width="100%">

### Key Insights

- Customers under 25 represented the largest segment.
- USA generated the highest revenue among all markets.
- Retail was the strongest-performing sales channel.
- Customer value varied significantly across age groups.

# 💡 Business Recommendations

### Pricing Strategy

- Maintain premium pricing for high-margin cocoa products.
- Reassess low-margin SKUs to improve profitability.

### Product Mix

- Expand high-performing product configurations.
- Prioritize profitable weight-cocoa combinations.

### Promotion Strategy

- Avoid excessive discounting.
- Focus on discount levels that maximize profit rather than volume.

### Customer Growth

- Develop targeted campaigns for high-value customer segments.
- Increase retention efforts in top-performing markets.

### Channel Optimization

- Continue investing in Retail channels.
- Explore growth opportunities in underperforming channels.
